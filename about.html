I am an aspiring data scientist/computation linguist with background in data science, cognitive science and linguistics who enjoys solving problems with data from an interdisciplinary perspective. I believe in the power of data science and artificial intelligence to transform our lives and make the world a better place.

I have a passion for languages and technologies. After working as research assistant at Cognitive Development Lab and Language Comprehension Lab, I found I am particularly interested in data analysis and computational linguistics, which provides an insight from computational perspective into thinking and intelligence.
